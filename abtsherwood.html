ABOUT SHERWOOD

Under the aegis of U.S. Srivastava Memorial Educational Society, Sherwood Educational Group began its journey way back in- 1989. Guided by its vision to establish world class educational institutions for aspiring students and equipping them with skills of life and produce good citizens of the country. To translate the vision into action the group always believed in the philosophy -"Our people are our biggest asset." It reflects in our core team of leaders. They all have proven track record of excellence in their respective fields.

ABOUT SHERWOOD EDUCATIONAL GROUP

Sherwood Educational Group is a chain of leading educational institutions in India established in the year 1989 under the aegis of U.S. Srivastava Memorial Education Society registered as nonprofit organization under the Society Registration Act 1860. The Educational Group offers holistic professional education in the fields of primary, management, computer application, commerce, pharmacy and medical education

Er. K. B. Lal, an Engineer by profession, a great visionary, educationist and philanthropist, in his conquest towards serving the society, initiated the mission of imparting quality, value and skill based education right from primary to higher professional level at highly affordable fee. Er. Lal has a strong and unassailable view to cater to that section of society who wants to send their children for primary, management, computer application, commerce, pharmacy and medical education but cannot afford to pay the high fees charged by the educational institutions run by big business houses. Thus, the fees remained, and continue to remain highly affordable.

SHERWOOD CAMPUS LUCKNOW

The Sherwood Campus Lucknow houses three establishments namely Sherwood College of Management, Sherwood College of Professional Management and Sherwood Academy.

SHERWOOD COLLEGE OF MANAGEMENT LUCKNOW

Sherwood College of Management offers two years full time Master of Business Administration (MBA) degree course recognized by AICTE, Ministry of HRD, Government of India, New Delhi and affiliated to Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University, Uttar Pradesh, Lucknow with an intake of 120 students.

Since its inception in1997, Sherwood College of Management, Lucknow has grown into a known business school in the field of management education. The Institute is working on the philosophy of imparting knowledge with values and ethics amongst the students. The Institute is A+++ rated by Business India.

WHY SCM?

  • 29+ years of legacy in Management Education.
  • Consistently 100 % placement to all the eligible candidates in renowned corporate houses.
  • Highly affordable fee structure with highest value for money in view of CTC offered.
  • Highly qualified and experienced faculty from reputed institutions like IITs, IIMs and the University of London.
  • Application-based learning : KAIZEN presentations, Case studies, Projects, Management Workshops, Business Plans and Industrial Visits.
  • Personality Development & Training Sessions (special focus on English Communication) to groom students for interviews. More than 2000 + alumni acting as mentors ushering the students to corporate India.
  • mong top 51 business schools as per Business India Survey. A+++ category among the best B Schools in India by Business India.
  • Multimedia classrooms and Wi-Fi enabled campus.
  • Lush green and pollution free campus located in the heart of the city.

SHERWOOD COLLEGE OF PROFESSIONAL MANAGEMENT LUCKNOW

Sherwood College of Professional Management offers five professional programmes at Graduation level courses -

  1. Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  2. Bachelor of Business Administration in Tourism (BBA — T)
  3. Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA)
  4. Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)
  5. Bachelor of Commerce Honors (B.Com - H)

All the courses, each with a sanctioned strength of 60 seats, are of full time three years duration affiliated permanently to University of Lucknow, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh.

Sherwood College of Professional Management, established in the year 1997 by U.S. Srivastava Memorial Education Society. The guiding objective of the college is to channelize the abundant energy and creativity of youth, right from the initial stages. Our commitment to the society and its faith give us the much needed impetus to overcome any hurdle that may creep up in our path.

SHERWOOD ACADEMY LUCKNOW

Sherwood Academy, Lucknow, established in 1989 under the aegis of the U.S. Srivastava Memorial Society, Lucknow, founded by the educationist Er. K. B. Lal, is affiliated to C.I.S.C.E New Delhi. In subsequent years some prestigious professional colleges were added in the feather of Sherwood Educational Group with the vision that a student starting from childhood education secures a professional degree from these institutions of the same group.

During the last 3 decades, the academy has produced several achievers in all fields. But this is not all. The academy has embarked upon a dynamic new-age programme ; where learning is both instructional and specifically ethno-spiritual. A co- educational entity, Sherwood Academy offers conducive, highly disciplined and clean environment for a child's holistic development. Poised to impart value-based, fundamentally strong education to a child, the Sherwood Academy is a reflection of its founder's abiding principles.

SHERWOOD CAMPUS, BARABANKI

The Sherwood Campus Barabanki houses four establishments namely Sherwood Hospital, Barabanki, Sherwood College of Nursing, Barabanki, Sherwood Institute of Paramedical Sciences, Barabanki and Sherwood College of Pharmacy, Barabanki.

SHERWOOD HOSPITAL, BARABANKI

Sherwood Hospital, Barabanki, is registered with the Department Medical, CMO office, Barabanki and run by the US Srivastava Educational Society, which is a non-profit charitable organization. Healthcare Services are available to all at affordable prices to the people of Barabanki and adjoining areas. The most distinct, most well-equipped hospital with all modern facilities is available to you 24x7 round the year.

SPECIAL POINTS OF SHERWOOD HOSPITAL

  • Best patient testing and consultation by senior doctors in all departments.
  • Free consultation facility to the bound patient.
  • The facility of testing of Paloji (blood, urine, mucus, semen, feces etc.) at very low prices by state-of-the-art machine.
  • Ultrasound and digital X-ray facility at the lowest price
  • Air-conditioned (AC) beds.
  • Provision of 24-hour facility of ventilator and photo therapy machine etc. for a newborn.
  • Supply of RO water to the entire hospital by the Eureka Forbes.
  • Provision of nutritious food for admitted patients.
  • 24x7 controlled power system of the entire complex by large generators.
  • High level security system.
  • Facilitated cleanliness of the entire campus.
  • 24-hour emergency and trauma center facilities are available.
  • Trauma Center, 24-hour emergency, ambulance facility.

SHERWOOD COLLEGE OF NURSING BARABANKI

Sherwood College of Nursing, Barabanki offers four years B. Sc. Nursing degree course recognized by Indian Nursing Council, New Delhi. The Institute has initially a sanctioned intake of 40 seats. The course is approved by the Uttar Pradesh State Medical Faculty, Lucknow, government of Uttar Pradesh. The Institute is affiliated to Atal Bihari Vajpayee Medical University, Lucknow.

SHERWOOD INSTITUTE OF PARAMEDICAL SCIENCES BARABANKI

Sherwood Institute of Paramedical Sciences, Barabanki offers five diploma courses viz. Diploma in Emergency and Trauma Care Technician, Diploma in Dialysis Technician, Diploma in Optometry Technician, Diploma in OT Technician and Diploma in Physiotherapy Technician. All the five courses are approved by Uttar Pradesh State Medical Faculty, Government of Uttar Pradesh, Lucknow.

These diploma courses are job oriented academic programmes. We, at SIPS, Barabanki teach an individual and change them into a competent health care worker in view of the realization of the significance of paramedical personnel in the health sector. Health care delivery systems are undergoing fast changes and the demand for expert paramedical personnel, the paramedics, is on the rise. This, in turn, has increased the demand for Paramedical courses in recent times.

SHERWOOD COLLEGE OF PHARMACY

Approved by Pharmacy Council of India & AICTE, Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India, New Delhi, and affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Uttar Pradesh, Lucknow, it offers 4-years full time Degree Programme - B. Pharm and 2-years Diploma Programme - D. Pharm, recognized by Board of Technical Education, Uttar Pradesh,. The courses provide extensive knowledge in the subjects like Anatomy, Physiology, Pharmaceutical Jurisprudence, Pharmacognosy, Pharmaceutics, Pharmaceutical Microbiology, Pharmaceutical Industrial Management. The electives include; Drug Design; Clinical Pharmacy and Drug Interactions; Pharmaceutical marketing; Pharmaceutical Packaging; Novel Drug Delivery System; and GMP, Quality Assurance & Validation.
