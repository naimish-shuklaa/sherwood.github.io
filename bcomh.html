OVERVIEW

The Bachelor of Commerce with Honours [B.Com.(Hons)] degree is an undergraduate programme which has been specially designed for meritorious students who scored minimum 60% marks in Intermediate along with Maths/ Economics/ commerce as one of the compulsory subjects. The program is the combination of commerce and management as well. The degree involves intensive study, focuses on application-based education and culminates with the completion of a research dissertation as the course is affiliated to University of Lucknow. The Honors programme is a three year full- time study. One of the most suited options for B.com (Honors) graduates is taking Chartered Accountancy or CPA exams, enabling them to get jobs in the field of taxation, audit or accountancy.


PROGRAM OUTCOMES(POS):

P0I: This program could provide Industries, Banking Sectors, Insurance Companies, Financing companies, Transport Agencies, Warehousing etc., well trained professionals to meet the requirements.

P02: After completing graduation, students can get skills regarding various aspects like Marketing Manager, Selling Manager, over all Administration abilities of the Company.

P03: Students can independently start up their own Business.

P04: Students can get thorough knowledge of finance and commerce.

P04: Essential skills for corporate

ABOUT SHERWOOD

Under the aegis of U.S. Srivastava Memorial Educational Society, Sherwood Educational Group began its journey way back in 1989. Guided by its vision to establish world class educational institutions for aspiring students and equipping them with skills of life and produce good citizens of the country. To translate the vision into action the group always believed in the philosophy -"Our people are our biggest asset." It reflects in our core team of leaders.
