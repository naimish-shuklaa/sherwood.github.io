Sherwood College is a renowned educational institution that places great emphasis on providing a holistic education to its students. In addition to academic excellence, the college believes in organizing various events throughout the year to help its students develop their personality, showcase their talent, and become well-rounded individuals. Some of the events that the college organizes are Fresher Day, Orientation of Fresher, Republic Day, Independence Day, Sports events, competition events, workshops, webinars, seminars, and many others.

Fresher Day is an event that is organized at the beginning of the academic year to welcome new students to the college. It is a fun-filled day that includes cultural performances, games, and various other activities to help the new students feel comfortable in the new environment. The event is an opportunity for the seniors to interact with the freshers and make them feel at home.

The Orientation of Fresher is an event that is organized to help the new students understand the rules and regulations of the college. The event is usually organized by the faculty members and provides an opportunity for the students to interact with them and get a better understanding Of the academic curriculum of the college.

Republic Day and Independence Day are two important national events that are celebrated with great enthusiasm in Sherwood College. These events are an opportunity for the students to showcase their patriotism and love for the country. The events are usually organized with flag hoisting, cultural performances, and speeches that inspire the students to contribute to the development of the nation.

Sherwood College also organizes various sports events throughout the year. These events are an opportunity for the students to showcase their physical abilities and develop a healthy competitive spirit. The college also organizes various competition events such as debates, quizzes, elocutions, and other such events. These events provide an opportunity for the students to showcase their intellectual abilities and develop their skills in various fields.

In addition to these events, Sherwood College also organizes various workshops, webinars, and seminars throughout the year. These events are organized to provide the students with a platform to learn from experts in various fields and gain knowledge about the latest developments in their respective fields.

In conclusion, Sherwood College is committed to organizing various events throughout the year to help its students develop their personality and become well-rounded individuals. These events provide an opportunity for the students to showcase their talent, develop their skills, and contribute to the society. The college places great emphasis on organizing these events with great enthusiasm and zeal every year.

SHERWOOD COLLEGE OF NURSING

The Sherwood College of Nursing, a unit of U.S Srivastava Memorial Educational Society is situated in its Barabanki campus. Our institution is established under the visionary of Honorable Chairman Er. K.B Lal: in a view to meet the needs of existing and future health care demand. Our institution is affiliated to Atal Bihari Bajpayee Medical University, UP recognized by Uttar Pradesh State medical faculty and approved by Uttar Pradesh State Government. Our goal is to provide the best "Education and Clinical Training" in the field of nursing.
